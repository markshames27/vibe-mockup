# VirtualPainter - Lean Canvas

**Project**: Paint Color Visualizer (VirtualPainter)  
**Date Created**: December 15, 2025  
**Workflow**: Vibe Mockup - Skill 1  
**Target Market**: Painting Companies (B2B SaaS)

---

## 1. Problem

**Top 3 Problems:**

1. **Homeowners struggle to visualize** how different paint colors will look on their actual house before committing to a purchase
2. **Painting companies lose sales** because customers are uncertain and delay decisions or choose competitors
3. **Physical paint samples are inconvenient** - require time, effort, and don't show the full picture

**Existing Alternatives:**
- Physical paint chips and sample pots (time-consuming, limited)
- Generic color visualization tools (not personalized to their house)
- Photoshop/manual editing (requires expertise, expensive)

---

## 2. Customer Segments

**Primary Customer**: Small to medium-sized painting companies

**Characteristics:**
- Have an existing website
- Want to differentiate from competitors
- Serve residential homeowners
- Looking for tools to increase conversion rates
- Willing to invest in technology that drives sales

**Secondary Users**: Homeowners (end users of the tool via painting company websites)

---

## 3. Unique Value Proposition

**Single, Clear Message:**

> "Let your customers see their house in any color—instantly. Close more deals with AI-powered paint visualization."

**Why It's Different:**
- Uses AI (Gemini 2.5 Flash) for intelligent, realistic color replacement
- Embeddable widget for painting company websites
- No technical expertise required for homeowners
- Instant results—upload photo, see new colors in seconds
- Focuses specifically on exterior house walls

---

## 4. Solution

**Core Features:**

1. **Photo Upload**: Homeowners upload a picture of their house
2. **AI Wall Detection**: Automatically identifies wall surfaces to recolor
3. **Predefined Color Palette**: Select from curated paint colors (painting company's offerings)
4. **Real-time Preview**: See the house with new colors instantly
5. **Save & Share**: Download or share the visualization
6. **Webview Embeddable**: Easy integration into painting company websites

**High-Level Concept:**
A simple, embeddable widget that painting companies add to their website. Homeowners use it to visualize paint colors on their own house photos, increasing confidence and driving purchase decisions.

## 7. Cost Structure

**Key Costs:**

1. **AI Processing**: OpenRouter API costs for Gemini 2.5 Flash (per image processed)
2. **Hosting & Infrastructure**: Server costs, database, CDN for images
3. **Development**: Initial build and ongoing maintenance
4. **Marketing & Sales**: Customer acquisition costs
5. **Support**: Customer service for painting companies

**Cost Optimization:**
- Tier pricing ensures heavy users pay more
- Efficient image processing to minimize API costs
- Self-service onboarding to reduce support costs
